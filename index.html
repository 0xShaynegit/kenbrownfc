About Ken

Ken is a wealth management consultant specializing in serving American expats and international business professionals. With extensive experience across Dubai, the United States, and now Thailand, Ken brings a unique global perspective to wealth management and financial planning.

Based in Thailand and leveraging his deep understanding of international tax implications, Ken helps clients navigate complex financial situations and optimize their wealth strategies using the benefits of the US/Thailand tax treaty.

Ken's expertise encompasses retirement planning for American expats, investment strategy, trust services, pension planning, and comprehensive estate planning designed specifically for the unique needs of expats.
